With all the various policies and guidelines associated to money sending services, we know from our own personal experience that sending money abroad is a rather challenging task to undertake. Whether you are studying or working in a foreign country, purchasing a property abroad or making large investments of any sort in any country the major concern you face is how to locate the perfect company that can handle your particular sums of transfers without any hitch in services and at the best rates affordable. That's why we developed MoneyScanner: the tool to provide you with the list of the existing remittance services compared at a single place. Problem solved, money saved!

Remittance is a complex method to comprehend. The sole most significant factor leading to lofty remittance rates is a lack of transparency in the market. It is complicated for consumers to compare rates because there are quite a number of variables that structure remittance prices. Prices for remittances are often made up of a fee charged for transferring a certain sum of money, a margin charged on the exchange rate when remittances are sent and received in various currencies, and, sometimes, the recipient of the funds is charged a certain fee. These fee workings may also fluctuate based to how the receiver is paid (i.e. by crediting an account or in cash), the possibility of the sender providing the recipient’s information (i.e. bank account details) and the transfer speed.
