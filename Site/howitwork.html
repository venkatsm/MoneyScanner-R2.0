How it works

MoneyScanner is a free and easy-to-use platform which helps you to make an international transaction of any sort.

  • Simply input the money equivalent to be sent and click GO (or click on the button at the end of this page)
  • MoneyScanner will immediately display a current list of remittance providers, their transfer rates, time frame and service charges
  • You can sort the results by various preferences according to your needs

We did all the hard work and always provide you with the updated information from all of the best existing money transfer services.
